The exception occurs rarely but has high impact as it prevents onboarding completion.

#### Trigger Conditions

> *What causes this exception to occur?*

The exception is triggered when the KYC (Know Your Customer) check, performed during the activation phase (Day 2), identifies that the customer operates in the defence industry. This is a compliance-driven gate that prevents onboarding of customers in restricted industry sectors.

#### Detection Method

> *How is this exception identified?*

Automated KYC screening system flags defence industry classification based on customer business registration details and industry codes.

#### Business Impact Analysis

> *What happens when this exception occurs?*

**Immediate Impact:**
- Onboarding journey is immediately terminated
- Customer does not receive standard activation communications
- Decline email is sent to customer

**Downstream Effects:**
- No further touchpoints are scheduled
- Customer relationship does not proceed
- 180-day journey is cancelled

**Client Impact:**
- Customer receives decline notification
- Cannot proceed with BizBanking services

**Regulatory/Compliance Risk:**
- Ensures compliance with banking regulations regarding restricted industries
- Mitigates risk exposure for the bank

#### Current Handling Procedure

> *Step-by-step: What happens today when this exception is triggered?*

1. KYC check runs during PS-ONB-002 (Activation Communications)
2. System identifies defence industry classification
3. Standard activation flow is halted
4. Decline email is automatically generated and sent to customer
5. Onboarding journey is terminated and marked as declined

#### Improvement Opportunities

> *How could this exception be prevented, detected earlier, or handled more efficiently?*

- Consider moving KYC check to PS-ONB-001 (Sign In) to avoid initiating journey for ineligible customers
- Review if defence industry exclusion is absolute or has exceptions for certain sub-sectors
- Ensure decline communication includes information on appeal process (if applicable)
