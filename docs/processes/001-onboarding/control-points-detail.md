#### Control Description

> *What does this control do? What risk does it mitigate?*

This preventive control screens new BizBanking customers during the activation phase to identify those operating in the defence industry. When detected, the control prevents onboarding from proceeding by terminating the journey and sending a decline notification. This ensures compliance with banking regulations regarding restricted industry sectors.

#### Regulatory/Policy Requirement

> *What regulation or policy mandates this control?*

**Regulation/Policy:** KYC/AML Regulations - Restricted Industry Screening

**Specific Requirement:** Banks must screen customers against restricted industry lists and decline services to those in prohibited sectors.

#### Control Activity

> *How is this control performed?*

Automated KYC screening checks customer industry classification against restricted industries list. Defence industry classification triggers process termination and decline email.
