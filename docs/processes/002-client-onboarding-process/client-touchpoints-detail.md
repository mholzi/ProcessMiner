## Executive Summary

The Client Onboarding journey comprises 8 client touchpoints across 5 stages, with a total Client Effort Score (CES) of 38.0. The heaviest effort burden falls in Stage 2 (Submitting My Application), which accounts for 58% of total CES due to document gathering and follow-up activities. Human-assisted touchpoints dominate (6 of 8), reflecting the manual, relationship-driven nature of the current process.
