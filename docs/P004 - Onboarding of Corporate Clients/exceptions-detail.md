## Detailed Exception Analysis

### EX-OCC-001: Defence Client Additional Approval

#### Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Exception ID** | EX-OCC-001 |
| **Name** | Defence Client Additional Approval |
| **Category** | Regulatory/Compliance |
| **Affected Steps** | All steps (PS-01-001 onwards) â€” full process hold |
| **Frequency** | Occasional (defence sector clients) |
| **Impact Level** | High |
| **Owner** | KYC Team |
| **Resolution Time** | 3-5 business days |

#### Trigger Conditions

The exception is triggered when:

- Client is identified as operating in the defence industry/sector
- Identification occurs at intake/registration stage

#### Business Impact Analysis

| Impact Area | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Immediate** | Onboarding journey delayed by 3-5 business days |
| **Downstream** | All 13 process steps postponed until approval |
| **Client Experience** | No communications received during review period (full hold) |
| **Compliance** | Required for regulatory compliance with defence sector requirements |

#### Current Handling Procedure

1. Defence sector client identified at intake
2. Standard onboarding journey paused (no communications sent)
3. Case escalated to KYC Team for additional approval
4. KYC Team reviews and approves/rejects (3-5 business days)
5. If approved: Standard journey begins from Day 1
6. If rejected: Client notified, onboarding terminated

#### Escalation Path

| Level | Owner | Timeframe | Action |
|-------|-------|-----------|--------|
| L1 | KYC Team | 3-5 days | Standard review and approval |
| L2 | Senior KYC Manager | If complex | Escalation for edge cases |

#### Root Cause Analysis

Defence sector clients require enhanced due diligence due to regulatory requirements around sanctions, export controls, and heightened risk profiles associated with the defence industry.
