## 3. Tenets

> _Guiding principles that inform all innovation decisions. Non-negotiable._

### Core Principles

1. **Innovation Must Align with Business Strategy**
   Focus on bank's strategic priorities. Innovation for innovation's sake is waste. Every recommendation must serve business objectives.

2. **Learn from Leaders, Differentiate from Competitors**
   Study industry leaders, analyze competitors, benchmark best practices. But differentiation, not imitation, creates competitive advantage.

3. **Client-Centric Innovation**
   Improve client experience or enable new client value. Internal efficiency alone is not sufficient justification.

4. **Proof of Concept Before Scale**
   AI, blockchain, RPA are promising but need validation. Pilot before full implementation. Validate assumptions before scaling.

5. **Regulatory Compliance Constrains Innovation**
   Banking innovation must work within regulatory boundaries. Understand constraints before proposing solutions.

6. **Feasibility Before Priority**
   Assess technical, business, strategic, resource, risk, and customer feasibility before prioritizing initiatives.
